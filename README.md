### The Renaissance of Prediction Markets

随着 Polymarket 等平台的爆发，预测市场已被证明是发现真理和聚合群体智慧的强大工具。然而，随着生态系统的成熟，市场结构中一个根本性的缺陷已无可辩驳：**资金效率低下**。

## 🚨 2. The Problem: The "Idle Capital" Dilemma

In traditional prediction markets, when a user purchases a position (Yes/No shares), their capital is locked in a smart contract until the event creates an outcome.

### 痛点：“资金闲置”困境
在传统的预测市场中，当用户购买仓位时，其资金会被锁定在智能合约中，直到事件结果揭晓。

### Key Issues (核心问题)

*   **📉 Zero Yield on Duration**
    Whether the event settles in 24 hours or 24 months, the locked capital earns **0% interest**. This contradicts the fundamental financial principle of the **Time Value of Money (TVM)**.
    > **久期零收益**：无论事件在24小时还是24个月后结算，锁定的资金都获得 0% 的利息。这违背了“货币时间价值”的基本金融原则。

*   **🛑 Discouraging Long-Term Betting**
    This opportunity cost creates a massive friction for long-duration markets (e.g., elections, climate goals, long-term crypto prices). Users are financially penalized for locking up liquidity, leading to thin order books and lower accuracy for long-term predictions.
    > **阻碍长期预测**：这种机会成本为长周期市场制造了巨大的摩擦。用户因锁定流动性而受到财务上的“惩罚”，导致长期预测市场的订单薄变薄，准确度下降。
